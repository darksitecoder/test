New Start-Ups Service

4 Corners TDS offers personalized one-on-one support for new company start-ups. If you're interested in becoming an owner-operator, we'll help you achieve your goals. Starting a new company can be overwhelming, especially when selecting the right equipment for your routes and lanes. Our experienced dispatchers and planners will guide you in choosing the best equipment for your needs, ensuring you're home on time as requested.

Our comprehensive start-up service includes:

  • Federal Authority Setup: We'll guide you through the process of obtaining your federal authority.
  • License Plate Registration: We'll handle the paperwork and registration for your license plates.
  • Truck and Trailer Sourcing: We'll help you find the perfect truck and trailer for your business needs.
  • Insurance Setup: We'll connect you with reputable insurance companies to ensure you're fully covered.
  • Lease Company Matching: We'll find a suitable company for you to lease under.
  • CDL Driver Sourcing: If you don't hold a CDL license, we can find a qualified driver for you.
  • Team Driver Matching: If you're a solo driver looking to run as a team, we can find a compatible partner.
  • Over-the-Road Training: Our professional drivers will train you on the job, ensuring you're equipped for success.
