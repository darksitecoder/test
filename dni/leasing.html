Leasing Program

  • 90% Pay of Gross Income: Maximize your earnings.

Our Leasing Services

Lease-to-Own Options

We consider that every driver is entitled to a road to ownership. Our lease to own programs are set to do just that, easy, even, and do-not-need based. You will be able to select the type of truck well maintained, to select a payment plan that best fits your budget and you will be able to drive knowing that there will not be any hidden fees or unexpected punishment. We will assist you to see the terms of contract, to comprehend the duties and to be safe that your truck investment is really profit oriented to your long-range purposes.

Maintenance & Support

Placing a truck on lease is not only about being in the driver seat but it is also about remaining on the road without inconveniences. That is why our program consists of proactive maintenance schedules and discounted repair partnering. We also assist in dealing with the breakdowns, inspection, and compliance checks in order to reduce downtimes and unforeseen expenses. Our 24/7 service and professional help ensure that your equipment is always reliable, safe, and roadworthy.

Mileage/ Fuel Economy & Safety

We are concerned with profitability and performance. That is the reason why all trucks in our leasing business are rigorously tested in the context of fuel efficiency and safety standards. You will operate a car that is not only fully insured, up to date and fitted with latest technology to make you save on fuel and remain in line with the federal requirements. Our dispatch team too will assist in planning efficient routes that will be fuel efficient, to make your operations lean and economical.

Driver Success Partnership

When you lease through us, it is not just a truck but a network that was created to make your success. Our dispatchers pay preference to leased operators who have steady, high-paying loads, and we offer business coaching continuously on how to handle costs, routes, and size. We manage all partnerships as a lifelong investment to your development and self-sufficiency in the trucking business.
