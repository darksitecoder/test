Transition to Flatbed Hauling with Confidence

Our specialized service is designed for dry van owner-operators looking to transition to flatbed hauling. We understand that flatbed operations require strict safety protocols and inspections to protect public safety, minimize insurance claims, and prevent accidents and cargo damage.

Our one-on-one flatbed training program includes:

  • Assigned Flatbed Instructor Driver: Get guidance and support to secure your first five loads.
  • Experienced Dispatcher: Work with a dispatcher who has flatbed driving experience to ensure smooth operations.
  • Live Video Inspections: Receive strict, one-on-one guidance with live video inspections every two hours to ensure compliance with safety standards.
  • Tarp Training: Master the skills needed for secure cargo transport.
  • Flatbed Tool Procurement: We help you purchase the necessary tools at competitive prices.
  • Flatbed Trailer Rental/Purchase Assistance: Get support in finding the right flatbed trailer for your needs.
